# Kwonseonki Office Design System

## 1. Product
Local law office website for debt consultation, personal rehabilitation, bankruptcy, immunity review, seizure response, and collection response.

## 2. Visual Tone
Serious, calm, premium, and practical. The site should feel like a focused legal office, not a broad law-firm advertisement.

## 3. Color Tokens
- Primary blue: `#1769d1`
- Primary blue hover: `#1f75df`
- Ink: `#101927`
- Dark navy: `#071523`
- Dark surface: `#2c3138`
- Light surface: `#f5f7fa`
- Border: `#d5dde8`
- Muted text: `#667386`

## 4. Typography Tokens
- Display: 40-48px, 800 weight, compact line-height.
- Heading: 28-34px, 800 weight.
- Body: 16-18px, 500-600 weight, 1.65 line-height.
- Label: 14-16px, 700 weight.

## 5. Spacing Tokens
Use a 4px rhythm. Section spacing is controlled by viewport-aware CSS variables and should leave room for the fixed header, floating banner, and bottom consultation bar.

## 6. Components
- Header pill navigation: fixed, rounded, dark surface with primary-blue consultation CTA.
- Floating side banner: fixed right-side quick actions with blue local assets.
- Bottom consultation bar: fixed quick form, dark surface, blue submit CTA.
- Section panels: full-width bands or unframed layouts, no nested card stacks.
- Case/status list: legal-document inspired rows, not decorative badges.

## 7. Copy Rules
Use concrete, verifiable wording. Avoid guarantee-like claims. Do not present win rates or success rates unless legally reviewed and substantiated. Prefer experience, process, consultation scope, and current situation checks.
